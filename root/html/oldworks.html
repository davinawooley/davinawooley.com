Experiments

I refer to my creative works as a collection of experiments because my goal is to explore the potential of innovative technologies to develop user-centered solutions. Via the amalgamation of design with development, I leverage various mediums and modalities to explore effective and exciting ways to educate, entertain and assist users.

These experiments represent years of fun, a bit of frustration, and a lot of growth. Enjoy! :)
